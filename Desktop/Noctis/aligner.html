Invisalign (2021)

Challenge
Although the Porsche online configurator allowed almost unlimited options, the freedom wasn't without drawbacks. Buyers were often overwhelmed when they tried to set up their configuration. Without a simple and clear starting point, customers became confused, adding more time to the configuration process and reducing their overall satisfaction.

Solution
During my internship, I proposed the "Discover Design Recommendations" concept. A new way to facilitate the customer journey through the configuration process. This feature was made possible with the introduction of the pre-configured builds, from a selection of winning mixtures and the dealer's suggestions by hand. The customers could then:

Pick a recommendation and adjust it further to match the requirements of their own.

Utilize recommendations as their baseline before deviating from them.

Disregard recommendations completely and begin fresh.

The layout was balanced between guidance and freedom, giving the users the feeling that they were led without being restricted in their creative work.

Impact
The feature brought a more inviting and natural point of departure, making it possible for customers to arrive at a gratifying first configuration faster than before and eliminating the decision fatigue problem. Early testing and dealer feedback pointed to various effects, among which are:

Customers were more inspired and confident when they began the process.

The configurator was more accessible to first-time users, leading to a greater satisfaction overall.

Dealers noticed that after engaging with the recommendations, customers became more informed and decisive.

By rethinking the start of the configuration journey, the "Discover Design Recommendations" section enabled the Porsche configurator to be more inviting, efficient, and interactive.
